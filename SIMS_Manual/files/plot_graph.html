Plotting a Graph

Step 1

From the "Summary Window", select the appropriate X field from the drop-down box.


Step 2

Select the appropriate Y field from the drop-down box, then hit the "Graph" button
Tip: To plot the standard error (where applicable), select the appropriate standard error field from the drop-down box next to the drop-down box for both X and Y fields respectively.
Tip: You may plot more than one graph at a time.
